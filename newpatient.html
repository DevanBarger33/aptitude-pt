  When to Arrive for Your First Appointment

For your first appointment at Aptitude Physical Thearpy, please arrive half an hour before your appointments time to finish the patient registration process. You may print off and fill out the attached documents to bring to your first appointment if you wish.

Print Patient Registration Form.

  What to Bring to Your First Appointment

At your first appointment at Aptitude Physical Therapy, please bring the following information to help the Aptitude Physical Therapy staff to file your insurance claims.

  • Health Insurance Card
  • Driver's License or other proof of identity

Please note that while the Aptitude PT staff will help with filing claims, patients are responsible for all charges.
