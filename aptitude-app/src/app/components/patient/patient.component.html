Choosing Your Physical Therapist

Missouri state law requires a doctor's referral for physical therapy. Patients reserve the right to choose a specific physical therapy clinic or physical therapist. Ask us if you need more information about obtaining a referral.

When to Arrive for Your First Appointment

How Much Will It Cost?

Costs are different for different patients and different treatments. After your initial evaluation, your physical therapist will be able to estimate the usual charges and average length of treatment for a similar condition. You may ask to see a list of charges and ask for an explanation of how charges are billed. Our office staff is available to discuss charges, payment options, and insurance reimbursements. Please bring a copy of your insurance card, and we will help file your insurance claim.


If you do not have health insurance or if you prefer to pay out of pocket, our self-pay rates are as follows:


  • $70 - Initial Evaluation
  • $55 - Per Session (after initial evaluation)

What to Wear to Your Appointment...

Wear loose, comfortable clothing, especially if you will be in the exercise area. We do have hospital gowns available if you need them for evaluations, such as knee injuries. You can also bring clothes to change into since we have private rooms available.

Where to Find Us

Our office is located at 1342 East Primrose Street between National and Fremont in Springfield, Missouri. The easiest way to enter our parking lot is coming from the west to turn right because there is an island on Primrose at our entrance that prevents a left turn coming from the east.


Our office is on the main floor in Suite A.

Scheduling and Canceling Appointments

While we will need a referral from your primary doctor, you can call us to start the process. Please call our office at (417) 890-7787. You may also email at info@aptitudept.com.


If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please call our office 24 hours in advance. If you do not call within 24 hours of your appointment, we will still charge you for the appointment, in accordance to our office policy.
