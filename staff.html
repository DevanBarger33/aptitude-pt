Our Physical Therapists

Kelly Coleman PT, DPT, OCS, GCS

Board Certified Clinical Specialist in Orthopaedic Physical Therapy
Board Certified Clinical Specialist in Geriatric Physical Therapy

Kelly is glad to be back full time in an outpatient clinic, but he plans to continue teaching and researching. Kelly graduated from the University of Nebraska Medical Center and completed his Doctorate in Physical Therapy at Regis University. Kelly is Board Certified in Orthopedics and Geriatrics. He has over 20 years experience as a clinical instructor and has taught as an assistant professor in the classroom and as a clinical education instructor for physical therapists. Kelly enjoys teaching and helping patients overcome their problems with pain.

Terry Cox PT, DPT, OCS, FAAOMPT

Board Certified Clinical Specialist in Orthopaedic Physical Therapy
Fellow of the American Academy of Orthopedic Manual Physical Therapists

Terry is a graduate of the University of Missouri and Regis University where he completed his Doctorate in Physical Therapy. He completed his fellowship training in manual therapy through Regis University and is a Fellow of the American Academy of Orthopedic Manual Physical Therapists. He is Board Certified in Orthopedics and is a professor at Southwest Baptist University where he teaches orthopedics and manual physical therapy.

Terry has over 25 years of experience in treating patients with neurological/musculoskeletal pain and disorders. He enjoys teaching his patients and keeping current on the best available research.
